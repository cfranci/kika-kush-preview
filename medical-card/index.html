Medical patients

Your Colorado med card

Certification, the state portal, and what to bring — the whole path, start to finish.

Getting certified

New patients get certified through clinics like Medical Alternatives Clinic and Doctors of Natural Medicine. Once you’re certified, the state issues your card through the Colorado medical marijuana registry.

Sending us your physician certification

The Marijuana Enforcement Division requires med dispensaries to keep a copy of your physician certification on file. Pulling it takes two minutes:

  1. Log in at medicalmarijuana.colorado.gov.
  2. Click your name where your card status shows “Active.”
  3. Open the Provider Certification tab at the top of the page.
  4. Click your active certification in the list.
  5. Print or save it as a PDF (printer icon, upper right), then email it to kikakushmed@gmail.com.

At the counter

Bring

A valid Colorado driver’s license or state ID (myColorado digital ID works) and a physical or PDF copy of your med card.

Can’t accept

Passports on the med side, screenshots of your card, or laminated cards. When in doubt, bring the PDF.
