Know your flower

Good cannabis starts with knowing what you’re looking at. The basics below cover most of what a first-timer needs — and your budtender covers the rest.

Indica, sativa, hybrid

The classic shorthand for how a strain tends to feel — mellow, lifted, or somewhere in between. It’s a starting point, not a rulebook; your budtender can point you to the right shelf.

Terpenes

The aroma compounds that give each strain its personality — citrus, pine, gas, berries. If you know what you like to smell, you’re halfway to knowing what you like to smoke.

Start low, go slow

The golden rule, especially with edibles. Effects can take a while to arrive — give it time before going back for more. There’s no prize for rushing.

Concentrates & rosin

Potent, flavorful, and not just for veterans. We’ve carried rosin since our first shop opened — ask about EPC patient pricing if you’re a concentrates patient.

Your first visit

Bring a valid ID (21+, or 18+ with your CO med card) and don’t be shy. Everyone behind the counter loves a good question.

Med vs rec

Same building at Circle, two different counters and rule sets. Not sure which applies to you? The Q&A page breaks it down.
