Our story

A family dream,
built in the Springs.

Kika Kush is what happens when a family dream refuses to stay a dream.

The founder’s grandfather always dreamed of opening a dispensary. He didn’t get the chance — so his grandson took the dream and planted it in his hometown, Colorado Springs.

“I believe people should have the choice in what works best for them. I’m glad I’m able to provide an alternative to people in need.”
— THE FOUNDER

The mission has never moved: the best prices, a big variety, and excellent service. That’s it. That’s the whole trick.

Three openings in three years

  1. DECEMBER 2022Circle opens. The first shop, at 555 N Circle Drive — rosin on the shelf from day one.
  2. OCTOBER 2023Nevada opens. A second med shop at 3840 N Nevada Avenue, with a deep product lineup.
  3. APRIL 2025Rec side opens. Circle starts serving everyone 21+, all of Colorado Springs welcome.
