Q&A

Questions, answered

The stuff people actually ask, answered straight.

Do you have recreational?

Yes! Recreational is at our Circle location (555 N Circle Drive), open to everyone 21+. The Nevada location is medical only.

What are your hours?

Recreational: 10am–9pm daily, last call 8:45pm. Medical: 10am–7pm daily at both shops.

What ID works on the rec side?

A driver’s license or chauffeur’s license from any state, a military ID, a state ID card, or a passport. You must be 21+.

What do I need for the med side?

A valid Colorado driver’s license or ID card (passports can’t be accepted) and a physical or PDF copy of your med card. Screenshots and laminated cards can’t be accepted. See the med card guide for the full path.

Can I use the myColorado digital ID?

Yes! The myColorado digital ID works on both the medical and recreational side.

How does the loyalty program work?

You earn one point per dollar spent before tax. At 500 points you can redeem $25 in store credit — or keep stacking.

I’m an EPC patient — anything for me?

Welcome! We have special pricing for Eligible Patient Concentrates patients and bulk flower discounts. New EPC patients always welcome.

How does online ordering work?

Ordering happens on our Weedmaps storefronts — pick your shop on the order page. Tip for the Circle store: use the side filters on Weedmaps to switch between the recreational and medical menu.
