What kind of night is it?

Flower

The classic. Indica, sativa, and hybrid strains rotating on the shelf every week.

ChillSocialCreative

Ask for: indica-leaning for a mellow night, sativa-leaning for energy, hybrid for the middle path. Terpenes tell you the flavor — go by your nose.

First-timer tip: tell your budtender how you want to feel, not what you want to buy. That’s literally their favorite question.

Pre-rolls

Zero prep, all ritual. Singles and packs, rolled and ready.

SocialChill

Ask for: singles to try a strain cheap, packs when friends are coming over, infused when you want the volume turned up.

First-timer tip: a single pre-roll is the lowest-commitment way to find out what you like.

Browse pre-rolls on Weedmaps

Concentrates

Rosin, wax, and friends — we’ve carried rosin since the first shop opened.

CreativeExplore

Ask for: rosin if you care about flavor, cartridges if you care about convenience. Potent stuff — a little goes a long way.

Med patients: EPC patients get special concentrate pricing. Always ask.

Browse concentrates on Weedmaps

Edibles

Gummies, chocolates, and drinks. Slow to start, long to land.

ChillWind downSocial

Ask for: low-dose (2.5–5mg) if you’re new or it’s a social night, higher doses only once you know your number.

The golden rule: start low, go slow. Effects can take a while — don’t double up early.

Browse edibles on Weedmaps

Tinctures

Drops under the tongue — precise, smoke-free, and easy to measure.

Wind downExplore

Ask for: a dropper with clear dose markings and the THC:CBD ratio that fits how you want to feel.

Why people like them: exact doses, no smoke, kicks in faster than edibles.

Browse tinctures on Weedmaps

Topicals

Balms, lotions, and salves — cannabis for the skin, no high involved.

Wind downExplore

Ask for: balms for targeted spots, lotions for bigger areas. Non-intoxicating — you can use them any time of day.

Good to know: a favorite with the self-care-Sunday crowd.
